**Build better habits, stay motivated, and grow together.**

**KEY FEATURES**:

**[Smart Planning & Live Tracking]** Create custom study schedules effortlessly. With new Live Activities support, you can track your focus progress directly from your Lock Screen or Dynamic Island without any distractions.

**[Gamified Reward System]** Turn effort into tangible rewards! Earn points for completing tasks or maintaining streaks. Set up a custom "Reward Store" (e.g., "1 Hour of Gaming") to keep motivation high.

**[Parent & Co-Learning Mode]** A unique feature for families. Parents can switch to Administrator Mode to view progress, assign tasks, and approve rewards. Foster a healthy, transparent, and encouraging environment.

**[Advanced Learning Tools]**

* Audio Recording: Log thoughts or lectures during your study sessions.

* iCloud Sync: Keep your data safe and synced across all your devices.

* Reading Management: Log pages and manage your "To-Read" and "Finished" bookshelves.

**[Visualize Your Growth]** 

Join challenges like "7-Day Early Rise" and watch your efforts turn into beautiful data trends and achievement badges.
